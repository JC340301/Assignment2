Mission Statement

The goal of the website is to expand the business to a wider market and to raise product awareness to the consumers and client and inspire the kids’ world by providing a complete experience for today’s kids with exciting fashion lifestyle products. The client will have a better understanding and description about the products available and will make the appearance of the website more appealing to the customers.


Success Evaluation

  • Monthly visitorship to the website.
  • Increase of sales revenue prior to the establishment of the website.
  • Amount of purchases through website (Monetization).
  • Number of partnership offers.
  • Customer satisfaction of their experiences browsing the company website

Target Audience

  • Young kids and the parents.
  • Fun, bright colored themes
  • To carter to the target audience, we will have interesting animated figures in the body background.

Content

  • Products:

    This page will contain images and information about the product being sold. It will be divided into two sections, Beach Wear and Jeans. Under each section will contain the image of the product and some details about it, whereby selecting an image will lead to another page which give more detailed information regarding the product.


  • Cafe':

    This page will contain pictures and information regarding the upcoming cafe' shope. Also, there will be information regarding the opening date and promotions offered by the cafe'.


  • Events:

    This page will contain information regarding events of the outlet.


  • Forms:

    There will be two pages, which are Online Order Form and Online Feedback Form. Online Order Form will allow cusstomers to place their order of the product they would like to purchase and Online Feedback form will allow customers rate the company's services and provide feedback to make any improvements if needed.


  • About Us:

    There will be two sections, which are "Company Mission" and "Contact Us". Company page will provide infromation and a brief description about Frias, the company's vision and mission statement. Contact Us will contain the contact details of the company


  • Help:

    THere will be three sections, which are "FAQ", "Service Page" and "Chat Service". FAQ page will contain questions placed by the customers and answers given by the organization. Service page contians information regarding delivery details and free delivery. Chat Service page allows customer to talk face with the employees of the company to clarify any queries of the customers.
