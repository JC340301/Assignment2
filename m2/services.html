  • You may receive your order 3-5 working days after the day you make purchase

Value-added Service

We provide full body measurements to make sure your children get the best fit clothes. Children are playful and active, so we make sure they wear the most comfortable clothes to support their creativity and confidence. We wish this value-added service can enhance the satisfaction of our consumer and so children can feel more comfortable.

    Exchange and Return Policy

  • If something wrong and you wish to return or exchange your order, you can do it in our store. Don’t forget to bring the proof of your purchase.
  • If you wish to exchange your order:
    • It must be returned within 7 days with the proof of purchase
    • Make sure the original tag is still attached
    • The item has not been washed, and worn
    • You can only exchange your order with the same product as you purchased
  • If you wish to return your order, you can get your full refund if:
    • The item you received is different from the item you purchase
    • The item you received is defect
  • There is no additional fee for any exchange and return
