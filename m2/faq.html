Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Do you provide international shipping?

    Unfortunately, we do not provide international shipping for now. We only deliver throughout Singapore. But, we keep improving our service and we hope we can ship internationally in short time.

  2. Can I cancel my order after the payment?

    Once your order has been paid, we process it immediately and we are sorry you cannot cancel your order. However, in the event of delivery of wrong product or defects, refund or exchange or goods can be given.

  3. How long does it take to confirm my order?

    Once your payment has been completed, your order is confirmed and you will receive an email about your confirmation within 2 working days.

  4. What are the payment methods for online orders?

    Currently our company only accepts payments via PayPal.
