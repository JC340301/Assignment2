Usability Testing Results

Testing:

the testing of the website is done to see if the website is made to see if it meets the purpose of the website and it mathces with the taste and preferences of the target audience, which are young kids and children. In order to find out, a testing is done where people or groups are asked to test the website by going through the differnet pages. The testees will not be given guidance on how to navigate around the website and after they have finshed the testing, they can ask questions regarding the website. Also, the testees will fill a survey form where they will decribe their specific and overall expreience of the website. Also, they will provide feedback regarding the problems they find in the website and recommendations to solve them.



Findings:

A total of three testing was done. According to the testings, people has marked 2s and 3s, which is close to or is very satisfied. This indicates that the people have a positive response on the website. On the other hand, since no search engine was placed, there was no feedback regarding site's search facility. Also, the tops (shirts and t-shirts) were placed in the "Jeans" section and one of the groups who did the test stated the contrast between the heading and the text color in the content are off.



Summary:

From the findings, it can be concluded that the people or group that did the testing had an overall satisfaied experience in the use of the website. From the feedback provided, the main banner has been edited to avoid confusion of the company name and the slogan. Furthermore, the main heading of the product page that contains jeasn and shirts and t-shirts products have been changed from "Jeans" to "Jeasn and Tops".
